Let’s first get you set up with the skin conductance sensor. Our facilitator will put in on your fingers and wrist.

You can use your mobile phone at any time. Please leave it on the piece of paper on the desk and keep a tally of each time you check it. You can choose whether to leave it screen up or down and change it at any time.

In this study, will be given a Java program to modify. You will also be answering questions based on the program you are given. You will be given 30 minutes to complete the tasks. Once the 30 minutes have elapsed, the study will end. You will receive on-screen notifications when there is 10 minutes left and when there is 2 minutes left.

We designed the tasks so that they would be suitable for fourth year software engineering students and postgraduate computer-science students.

If you accidentally close the questionnaire, you may reopen it again by clicking located at the top right corner.

When you’re ready, press the start button on the on-screen timer.
